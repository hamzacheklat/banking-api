Each record may contain:
FLOW, TASK, JOB_ID, SUB_ID, FAILURE REASON, WHAT TO DO, START_TIME, END_TIME, Date, ORIGIN, TOWER_URL.

Important:
- The field "Date" indicates the date of the flow.
- Use this field to answer questions about flows on a specific date.
- Only consider STATUS or FAILURE REASON to determine if a flow failed.
